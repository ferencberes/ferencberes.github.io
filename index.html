Complex Networks 2018

I am going to be a spotlight session presenter at the Complex Networks 2018 conference. The title of our work is Node Embeddings in Dynamic Graphs. Our code and Twitter data sets are available in this GitHub repository.

PhD studies in Computer Science

In 2016, I have started my PhD studies in Computer Science at Eötvös Loránd University. My area of research is dynamic network analysis. My main focus is dynamic centrality measures and online graph embedding methods.

KDD Workshop 2018

This year we published our work on dyamic centrality measures in the Applied Network Science journal. The title of our contribution is Temporal walk based centrality metric for graph streams. I also presented the short version of this research at the 14th International Workshop on Mining and Learning with Graphs (KDD18).

Complex Networks 2017

I presented a poster related to my PhD research on the Complex Networks 2017 conference. The title of the poster is Online Centrality in Temporally Evolving Networks.
